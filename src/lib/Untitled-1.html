Bridging Research Across Borders

OncoIG AU is a specialized platform that connects oncology doctors and researchers in Saudi Arabia with talented medical students seeking research experience.

OncoIG AU منصة متخصصة تربط أطباء الأورام والباحثين في المملكة العربية السعودية بطلاب الطب المتميزين الباحثين عن خبرة بحثية.

Our mission is to accelerate oncology research by making collaboration easier — from posting requirements to matching and connecting the right people.
